About Me

I am an AI Engineer and Researcher focused on building systems that are not just powerful, but trustworthy, explainable, and genuinely valuable. With an MSc in Data Science & AI from Saarland University and hands-on industry experience, I bridge cutting-edge research with real-world product development.

My recent work at Digital Product School × SAP centred on one of enterprise AI’s biggest open challenges: how do you actually measure what an AI agent is worth to a business? I designed evaluation frameworks, multi-agent architectures, and business KPI mapping systems for a production-deployed platform — AgentCheck, now live at agent-check.org.

Beyond agentic AI, I have a strong background in AI safety: adversarial training, red teaming, membership inference attacks, and model robustness. I care about making AI systems that people can trust and that perform reliably in production.

I was also a Research Collaborator with Prof. Liaqat Majeed at NUCES-FAST Lahore on Theory of Automata and Machine Learning.
